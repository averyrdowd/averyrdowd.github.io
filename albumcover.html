Project 12:
Album Cover Typography



After listening to the album Random Access Memories by Daft Punk, I was inspired to create a sci-fi modern font. I created a grid and laid out each letter creating a typeface.

To take it a step further, a vinyl and cover were created. Although Daft Punk has a upbeat electronic sound, their lyrics are quite sad and romantic like in Instant Crush. After listening on repeat, I tried to incorporate the type in a new way by hand sewing the letters into paper. I followed the typeface translating it into stitches creating the cover.
