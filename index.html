[01] Silenced Cinema Tryptich Series

Silenced Cinema is a three day film festival in New York City showing films that are banned throughout the world. The event focuses on different topics such as language, race, sexual content, politics, and violence. Each tryptich focuses on the senses including: sight, speech, and sound.

Typographic tryptich. Made by cutting up words to create different shapes and intriguing negative spaces. Focusing on the words said that are blocked from being shared.

Symbolism tryptich. Collage of people symbolising the people whose voices have been silenced by censoring media.

Repetition tryptich. The striking blue eye shows the things that we want to see that are torn away from our sight.
