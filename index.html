Project 01: Silenced Cinema Tryptichs

Silenced Cinema is a three day film festival in New York City showing films that are banned throughout the world. The event focuses on different topics such as language, race, politics, and violence. Each tryptich focuses on the senses including: sight, speech, and sound.
