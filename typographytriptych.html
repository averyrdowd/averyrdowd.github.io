This work was part of a series consisting of three triptychs advertising a film festival only showing films that were banned around the world. Each poster represents a day of the festival and shares what will happen. The bright, bold terms noted focus on the overall theme of that day.

To complicate the message being relayed, the title was printed out, cut up, and pasted back together. The distorted words and letters create a censorship fracturing the phrase creating illegibility. The broken words created new shapes and spaces that were intriguing to explore. The information is spread out and searching is required to comprehend all of the information.
