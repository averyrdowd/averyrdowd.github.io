Project 06:
NYBG Through Poetry Book



After exploring New York City, three libraries were selected to create books on. Each book had to explore a topic that was found in the library or related to the library.

After visiting the New York Botanical Garden library in the Bronx, I was obsessed with the plant specimin books filling the shelves. As a way to get more in touch with nature, I created a poetry guide through the garden. Readers are encouraged to explore the grounds and visit each plant in the book to further delve into the litterature and the plant life.
