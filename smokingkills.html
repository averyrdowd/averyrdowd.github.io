Project 08:
Smoking Kills Motion



Smoking is an epidemic that has taken over the world, specifically at art schools. After being tasked with creating a PSA, I decided to look around me and see what I noticed. So many people of all ages smoke cigarette after cigarette ignoring the terrible graphic images on the boxes.

While brainstorming, I watched the movie Euro Trip. The intro credits had me glued to the screen in a elaborate infographic. After being inspired, I animated the PSA short Smoking Kills narrated by Mark Seeliger.
