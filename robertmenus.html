ROBERT Restaurant was in need of a new alternative menu. Years prior, a cover was used with a fastener for multiple pages. This was no longer practical for the restaurant which brought the challenge of creating a one page printable menu to easily and quickly replace.

ROBERT's existing personal identity was cleaned up and a strong, official brand guideline for menu and printed media was created.
