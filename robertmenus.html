Project 11:
ROBERT Menus Print



ROBERT Restaurant was in need of a new alternative menu. Years prior, a cover was used with a fastener for multiple pages. This was no longer practical for the restaurant which asked me to make a one page printable menu to easily and quickly replace.

Working closely with the General Manager, I cleaned up ROBERT's existing personal identity and created a brand guideline for menu and printed media.
